Tomato pasta

Description:

Tomato is the most famous Italian dish. Every Italian person has eaten it atleast once in his/her life.

It's usually made with tomato, basil and of course pasta.

Ingredients for 2 persons:

  • 160g of pasta;
  • a bootle of tomato sauce (around 500ml of tomato sauce);
  • oil, salt and basil to taste.

Steps:

  1. Put water and some salt in a pan and wait until the water boils;
  2. meanwhile, cook the tomato sauce with oil and basil in another pan;
  3. put some salt in the sauce while it cooks;
  4. once the water boils, throw in the pasta and wait until it's cooked;
  5. when the pasta is ready, drain it and put the sauce on top of it;
  6. mix pasta and sauce until they stick together;
  7. enjoy your tomato sauce! Buon appetito!
