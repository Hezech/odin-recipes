Carbonara

Description:

Carbonara is one of the most famous Italian dishes. It's from Rome, the capital of Italy, in the Lazio region.

It's usually made with pecorino romano, bacon, eggs and of course pasta.

Ingredients for 2 persons:

  • 160g of pasta;
  • 2 yolk eggs, 1 whole egg;
  • 50g of bacon;
  • 50g of pecorino romano;
  • pepper to taste.

Steps:

  1. Put water and some salt in a pan and wait until the water boils;
  2. meanwhile, cook the bacon in another pan;
  3. mix eggs, pecorino and pepper;
  4. once the water boils, throw in the pasta and wait until it's cooked;
  5. when the pasta is ready, drain it and put the bacon and the egg cream on top of it;
  6. mix pasta, cream and bacon untill they all stick together;
  7. enjoy your carbonara! Buon appetito!
