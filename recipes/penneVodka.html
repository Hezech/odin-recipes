Penne alla vodka

Description:

Penne alla vodka is a delicious dish. It's not sure where it's from.

It's usually made with cream, salmon, tomato and of course pasta.

Ingredients for 2 persons:

  • 160g of pasta;
  • 200ml of cream;
  • 100g of salmon;
  • pepper and parmesan to taste.

Steps:

  1. Put water and some salt in a pan and wait until the water boils;
  2. meanwhile, cook the salmon in another pan;
  3. add cream, parmesan and pepper to the salmon;
  4. once the water boils, throw in the pasta and wait until it's cooked;
  5. when the pasta is ready, drain it and put in the same pan where the cream is;
  6. mix pasta, cream and salmon until it sticks together;
  7. enjoy your penne alla vodka! Buon appetito!
